Governance Structure

Our transparent and accountable leadership framework for effective community development

Board of Trustees

The highest decision-making body responsible for strategic direction, policy formulation, and overall governance.

  • Strategic planning and oversight
  • Policy formulation and approval
  • Financial oversight and accountability

Caucus General Assembly

Represents community interests, provides grassroots input, and ensures community participation.

  • Community representation and feedback
  • Conflict resolution and peacebuilding
  • Community mobilization and engagement

Management Committee

Implements Board decisions, manages day-to-day operations, and oversees project execution.

  • Project implementation and monitoring
  • Operational management and coordination
  • Financial management and reporting

Our Leadership Journey

Key milestones in our governance and leadership development

2018

Formation of Community Development Committee

Initial community meetings led to the formation of the first community development committee, laying the foundation for organized leadership.

2019

Legal Registration & Board Constitution

Successful registration as an Incorporated Trustee under CAMA and constitution of the first Board of Trustees with elected community leaders.

2020

Establishment of Caucus Assembly

Formation of the Caucus General Assembly to ensure grassroots participation and democratic representation in decision-making.

2021

Management Committee Formation

Establishment of the Management Committee to handle day-to-day operations and project implementation, enhancing organizational efficiency.

2022-Present

Governance Strengthening & Expansion

Continuous improvement of governance structures, establishment of standing committees, and expansion of leadership roles to accommodate community growth.

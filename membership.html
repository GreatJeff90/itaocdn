Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ITAUCDN membership

Who can become a member of ITAUCDN? +

Membership is open to:

  • Indigenes of Akpautong–Urua Okok communities
  • Residents living in the communities
  • Individuals with ancestral ties to the communities
  • Corporate organizations operating within the communities
  • Individuals with genuine interest in community development
What are the benefits of becoming a member? +

ITAUCDN members enjoy numerous benefits including:

  • Voting rights in community decisions
  • Access to capacity building programs
  • Networking opportunities with community leaders
  • Priority consideration for employment in community projects
  • Transparent access to project reports and financial statements
  • Participation in community development initiatives
How much does membership cost? +

Membership fees are structured as follows:

  • Regular Member: ₦5,000 per year
  • Active Member: ₦10,000 per year
  • Patron Member: ₦50,000 per year
  • Student/Senior Citizen: 50% discount on regular fee
  • Corporate Membership: Custom pricing based on organization size
How can I pay my membership fee? +

You can pay your membership fee through the following methods:

  • Bank Transfer: To ITAUCDN official account
  • Cash Payment: At designated collection points
  • POS Payment: Available at ITAUCDN office
  • Mobile Money: Through authorized agents

How long does the registration process take? +

The registration process typically takes 7-14 working days from submission to approval:

  • Application Review: 2-3 working days
  • Document Verification: 3-5 working days
  • Payment Processing: 1-2 working days
  • Certificate Issuance: 1-2 working days
  • Welcome Package Delivery: 2-3 working days

You will receive regular updates via email or SMS throughout the process.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership category? +

Yes, you can change your membership category at any time:

  • Upgrades take effect immediately upon payment of difference
  • Downgrades take effect at the next renewal period
  • No administrative fees for category changes
  • Pro-rated refunds may apply for downgrades
  • Contact membership@itaucdn.com for category change requests
What happens if I don't renew my membership? +

If you don't renew your membership:

  • Your membership becomes inactive after 30 days grace period
  • You lose voting rights and committee participation privileges
  • Access to members-only events and programs is restricted
  • You can reactivate within 6 months by paying outstanding fees
  • After 6 months, you must reapply as a new member
  • All previously earned benefits and recognition are preserved

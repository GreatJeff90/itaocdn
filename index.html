Our Vision

United, progressive, and self-sustaining communities

To build a united, progressive, and self-sustaining Akpautong–Urua Okok communities, anchored on inclusive development, transparent leadership, and shared responsibility.

OUR MANDATE

"To institutionalize community aspirations, translate collective intent into structured development action, and ensure that progress remains measurable, transparent, and enduring."

— Incorporated Trustee of Akpautong Urua Okok Communities Development Network

Our Story

2018

Community Formation

Initial community meetings and formation of development committee to address pressing community needs.

2019

Legal Registration

Incorporation as a Trustee under CAMA with CAC registration, establishing our legal framework.

2020-Present

Growth & Expansion

Implementation of multiple community projects, partnership development, and membership growth across communities.

Legal & Operational Framework

Legal Status

Incorporated Trustee registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) of Nigeria.

Operational Coverage

Akpautong–Urua Okok communities in Esit Eket Local Government Area, Akwa Ibom State.

Governance Structure

Board of Trustees, Management Committee, and Caucus General Assembly ensuring transparent decision-making.

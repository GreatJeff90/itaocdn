Building United, Progressive & Self-Sustaining Communities

"The Incorporated Trustee of Akpautong Urua Okok Communities Development Network exists to institutionalize community aspirations, translate collective intent into structured development action, and ensure that progress remains measurable, transparent, and enduring."

Driving community-led development initiatives that advance social welfare, critical infrastructure, economic empowerment, education, environmental stewardship, and peaceful coexistence across Akpautong–Urua Okok communities in Esit Eket LGA, Akwa Ibom State.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a united, progressive, and self-sustaining Akpautong–Urua Okok communities, anchored on inclusive development, transparent leadership, and shared responsibility.

Mission: To drive community-led development through structured programs in infrastructure, education, economic empowerment, and environmental stewardship.

Organizational Profile

Legal Status & Mandate

  • Legal Name: Incorporated Trustee of Akpautong Urua Okok Communities Development Network
  • CAC Registration: Incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA)
  • Operational Coverage: Akpautong–Urua Okok communities in Esit Eket LGA, Akwa Ibom State
  • Institutional Purpose: To institutionalize community development through structured, transparent frameworks

Our Development Approach

ITAOCDN adopts a community-first, participatory approach to development. We believe sustainable progress is achieved when communities are actively involved in identifying needs, designing solutions, and implementing projects.

Our framework emphasizes transparency, accountability, inclusivity, and measurable impact across all initiatives.

Historical Background

2018 - Formation

Initial community meetings and formation of development committee

2019 - Registration

Incorporation as a Trustee under CAMA with CAC registration

2020 - First Projects

Launch of initial community development projects in water and education

2021-2023 - Expansion

Growth in membership, partnerships, and project portfolio expansion

2024-Present - Institutionalization

Strengthening governance, transparency frameworks, and digital presence

Our Programs & Activities

Structured development initiatives across key community needs areas

Community Infrastructure

Development of essential community infrastructure including roads, community halls, and public facilities.

  • Road rehabilitation and construction
  • Community hall construction and renovation
  • Public lighting and security infrastructure

Water, Sanitation & Energy

Ensuring access to clean water, sanitation facilities, and sustainable energy solutions.

  • Borehole drilling and water treatment
  • Public toilet and sanitation facilities
  • Solar energy and renewable energy projects

Education & Training

Promoting educational access, quality, and skills development for community members.

  • Scholarships and educational support
  • Vocational skills training programs
  • Adult literacy and digital skills programs

Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable livelihoods and economic opportunities for youth and women.

  • Micro-enterprise development support
  • Agricultural value chain development
  • Business skills and financial literacy training

Health & Social Welfare

Improving community health outcomes and social support systems.

  • Health awareness and screening programs
  • Support for vulnerable groups (elderly, disabled)
  • Maternal and child health initiatives

Peacebuilding & Advocacy

Fostering peaceful coexistence and advocating for community interests.

  • Conflict mediation and resolution
  • Stakeholder engagement and advocacy
  • Community governance and participation

Membership Eligibility

Membership is open to all indigenes and residents of Akpautong–Urua Okok communities who share our vision for community development.

Membership Criteria

  • Must be an indigene or resident of Akpautong–Urua Okok communities
  • Commitment to community development principles
  • Willingness to participate in community activities
  • Adherence to organizational constitution and code of conduct

Membership Benefits

  • Participation in community decision-making
  • Access to capacity-building programs
  • Eligibility for community support programs
  • Networking opportunities with community stakeholders

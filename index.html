Incorporated Trustee of Akpautong Urua Okok Communities Development Network (ITAUCDN)

“The Incorporated Trustee of Akpautong Urua Okok Communities Development Network exists to institutionalize community aspirations, translate collective intent into structured development action, and ensure that progress remains measurable, transparent, and enduring.”

Driving community-led development initiatives that advance social welfare, critical infrastructure, economic empowerment, education, environmental stewardship, and peaceful coexistence across Akpautong–Urua Okok communities in Esit Eket LGA, Akwa Ibom State.

About ITAUCDN

Our Mission & Vision

We are dedicated to fostering sustainable development across Akpautong–Urua Okok communities through collaborative efforts, transparent governance, and community-driven initiatives.

Our vision is to create a model community network that balances cultural preservation with progressive development, ensuring prosperity for current and future generations.

Community-led development planning and implementation

Transparent resource management and accountability

Inclusive participation across all community segments

Our Core Values

Unity & Collaboration

We believe in the power of collective action and shared responsibility.

Transparency

All our processes and decisions are open and accountable to community members.

Sustainability

We implement projects that benefit both present and future generations.

Inclusivity

Everyone in the community has a voice and a role in our development journey.
